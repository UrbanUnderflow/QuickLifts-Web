# Partnership-Led Community Growth

Pulse’s north star is Partnership-Led Community Growth: becoming the fitness platform that the most energized, community-driven brands and spaces choose as their digital home. Instead of relying on paid acquisition, Pulse grows by partnering with the communities that are already alive — giving them a challenge layer to run real seasons, rituals, and shared blocks their people can actually complete together.

In practice, that means Pulse only wins when partners do. We help creators, gyms, and brands turn scattered followers into cohorts moving through clear, time-bound challenges with visible streaks and soft accountability. Partners bring the culture and the people; Pulse brings the infrastructure that keeps those people showing up together and makes the health of that community measurable for both sides.

**Primary Partner Lanes**

- **Brands** – Athletic and wellness brands (e.g., Nike, Gymshark, Oner Active, On Running) that want their campaigns and product drops to live inside real training experiences, not just ads.
- **Gyms & Studios** – Creator-led gyms, CrossFit boxes, and studio networks where members already show up for each other in person and need a digital layer to carry that energy through the week and across locations.
- **Run Clubs & Community Collectives** – Run clubs and other movement collectives where the group run or shared ritual is the product, and Pulse can turn routes, seasons, and race builds into shared challenge journeys.

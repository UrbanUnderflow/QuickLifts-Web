# Partner ICP Snippets – Partnership-Led Community Growth

Short, ready-to-drop copy blocks for different partner types. Use these in outreach emails, decks, or landing pages, and adapt details (numbers, timelines, names) to the specific partner.

---

## Brands

**1. Athletic / apparel brand (e.g., Gymshark-style)**  
"Pulse turns your next drop into a 30-day strength season your community can actually finish together. Your athletes host the block, customers join with a tap from your channels, and you see exactly how many people didn’t just see the campaign — they trained through it."

**2. Performance / footwear brand (e.g., On Running-style)**  
"From 21-day recovery resets to 6-week 5K builds, Pulse gives your runners a shared path instead of scattered workouts. Captains host the blocks, routes and sessions live where your community already is, and you get a clean view of challenge joins, streak weeks, and who’s race-ready."

**3. Recovery / wellness brand**  
"Instead of another ‘reset’ email, Pulse gives you a co-branded recovery challenge your community moves through together — light strength, walks, and mobility, guided by your ambassadors. The result is fewer burnout valleys, more weeks where people feel like they’re in it together with your brand in the middle of the ritual."

---

## Gyms / Studios

**1. Creator-led gym**  
"Pulse turns your 8-week squat cycle into a shared story your whole gym can step into. Members scan a code in class, join the block with a tap, and check off each week’s sessions together — giving your coaches a live view of who’s still in the crew and who needs a nudge."

**2. CrossFit box / strength-focused facility**  
"Your members already show up hard for each other in person. Pulse gives that same community a digital spine: daily check-ins, coach-led challenges, and visible streaks that keep people connected between classes so they’re not starting from zero every Monday."

**3. Multi-location studio or franchise**  
"With Pulse, your studios share a single challenge calendar, so a member in Brooklyn and a member in Austin feel like they’re in the same season. Local coaches host the blocks, your brand frames the story, and you can see how many members ride out the full block instead of dropping off after week two."

---

## Run Clubs

**1. City run club**  
"Pulse turns your Tuesday tempos and Saturday long runs into an actual season. Captains host a 6-week block, runners join straight from the club chat, and every week you see who’s logging miles, who’s on a streak, and who might need a check-in before race day."

**2. Brand-affiliated run community**  
"Your run community already wears the kit and shows up to events — Pulse gives them a shared challenge to move through between race photos. Think: a co-branded base miles season or race build where your pacing plans, routes, and product moments live inside one challenge everyone can see and finish together."

**3. Neighborhood or grassroots crew**  
"Pulse keeps your crew’s energy from being trapped in a single group run. Set up a simple weekly block — two runs plus one ‘do something that moves you’ day — and give people a way to show they’re still in the story even when life pulls them off the usual route."

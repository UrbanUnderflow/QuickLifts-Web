# Partner ICP Copy Snippets – Partnership-Led Community Growth

Short, ready-to-drop copy blocks for different partner types. Use these in outreach emails, decks, or landing pages, and adapt details (numbers, timelines, names) to the specific partner.

---

## Brands

1. **Athletic / apparel brand (Nike / Gymshark / Oner Active / On Running-style)**  
   "Pulse turns your next drop into a 30-day season your community can actually train through together. Your athletes host the block, customers join with a tap from your channels, and you see exactly how many people didn’t just see the campaign — they lived with it for four weeks."

2. **Performance / footwear brand**  
   "From 21-day recovery resets to 6-week 5K builds, Pulse gives your runners a shared path instead of scattered workouts. Captains host the blocks, routes and sessions live where your community already is, and you get a clean view of challenge joins, streak weeks, and who’s race-ready."

3. **Recovery / wellness brand**  
   "Instead of another ‘reset’ email, Pulse gives you a co-branded recovery challenge your community moves through together — light strength, walks, and mobility, guided by your ambassadors. The result is fewer burnout valleys, more weeks where people feel like they’re in it together with your brand at the center of the ritual."

4. **Supplement / performance nutrition brand**  
   "Pulse turns your product education into a real-life protocol your community can follow. Pair your stack with a 4-week strength or run block, let your athletes host the season, and watch how many people actually complete the protocol instead of skimming a label."

5. **Lifestyle / athleisure brand**  
   "Your community already wears the kit and tags you in their stories — Pulse gives them a shared challenge to live inside between drops. Think: a co-branded ‘movement month’ where daily check-ins, creator-led prompts, and weekend meetups all live in one place your people can see and finish together."

---

## Gyms / Studios

1. **Creator-led strength gym**  
   "Pulse turns your 8-week squat cycle into a shared story your whole gym can step into. Members scan a code in class, join the block with a tap, and check off each week’s sessions together — giving your coaches a live view of who’s still in the crew and who needs a nudge."

2. **CrossFit box / performance-focused facility**  
   "Your members already show up hard for each other in person. Pulse gives that same community a digital spine: coach-led challenges, daily check-ins, and visible streaks that keep people connected between classes so they’re not starting from zero every Monday."

3. **Cycle or HIIT studio**  
   "With Pulse, your studio can run 4–6 week challenge seasons that live beyond the front desk poster. Riders join with a tap, track their streaks across classes and at-home sessions, and you can see which coaches and time slots are keeping people in the story all the way to the final class."

4. **Multi-location studio or franchise**  
   "Pulse gives all of your locations one challenge calendar, so a member in Brooklyn and a member in Austin feel like they’re in the same season. Local coaches host the blocks, your brand frames the story, and you can see how many members ride out the full block instead of dropping off after week two."

5. **Specialty coaching crew (powerlifting, Olympic lifting, endurance coaching)**  
   "Pulse lets your coaches run structured training blocks that clients actually finish together. Share a 6–8 week cycle, let lifters or athletes join from anywhere, and use check-ins and streaks to keep everyone moving through the same story instead of losing people in one-off spreadsheets."

---

## Run Clubs

1. **City run club**  
   "Pulse turns your Tuesday tempos and Saturday long runs into an actual season. Captains host a 6-week block, runners join straight from the club chat, and every week you see who’s logging miles, who’s on a streak, and who might need a check-in before race day."

2. **Brand-affiliated run community**  
   "Your run community already wears the kit and shows up to events — Pulse gives them a shared challenge to move through between race photos. Think: a co-branded base miles season or race build where your pacing plans, routes, and product moments live inside one challenge everyone can see and finish together."

3. **Neighborhood or grassroots crew**  
   "Pulse keeps your crew’s energy from being trapped in a single group run. Set up a simple weekly block — two runs plus one ‘do something that moves you’ day — and give people a way to show they’re still in the story even when life pulls them off the usual route."

4. **University or campus run club**  
   "With Pulse, your campus crew can run 4–8 week seasons that survive midterms and breaks. Captains host the block, members check in from wherever they’re training, and you can see which routes, meetups, and rituals keep people connected to the club all semester."

5. **Performance-focused run team**  
   "Pulse lets your coaches turn training plans into shared campaigns your athletes can see and feel. Drop your race build into a challenge, have captains lead weekly check-ins, and use streaks and completion rates to spot who’s ready, who needs support, and how your team is moving through the block together."

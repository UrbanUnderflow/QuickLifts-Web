# Partnership-Led Community Growth

Pulse’s north star is Partnership-Led Community Growth: we grow by becoming the fitness platform that the most energized, community-driven brands, gyms, and run clubs choose as their digital home. For partners, that means Pulse isn’t a side campaign or another app in the stack — it’s the shared challenge layer where their athletes, coaches, and captains run real seasons their people can finish together, and where we measure growth in how many of those people keep coming back for the next chapter.

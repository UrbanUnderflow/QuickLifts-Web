# Partnership-Led Community Growth

Pulse’s north star is Partnership-Led Community Growth: we grow by becoming the fitness platform that the most energized, community-driven fitness brands and spaces in the world choose as their digital home. That means we don’t chase every logo; we focus on three lanes where community is already the core product — athletic brands, creator-led gyms and studios, and run clubs — and we give them one place where their people can train, recover, and show up together in ways that feel true to how they already move.

For brands, Pulse is the performance layer under the campaign — the space where product drops, ambassador stories, and race partnerships turn into real challenge seasons that customers can actually follow, finish, and remember. For gyms and studios, we extend the energy of the class floor into the rest of the week with 6–8 week blocks and weekly rituals that live as shared challenges instead of disappearing PDFs and whiteboard photos. For run clubs, we become the digital home for the season: base miles, race builds, and off-season crews all tied together in one place where runners can see the story of what their crew actually did together.

Partnership-Led Community Growth is our commitment to measure success in behavior, not just reach. When a partner chooses Pulse, the win is not a one-time spike in signups; it’s a durable rhythm of join, complete, return — more people entering each season, more of them finishing, and more of them raising their hand for the next chapter. If we do this well, every new partnership is both a distribution win and a signal that serious fitness communities trust Pulse to hold their culture without diluting it.

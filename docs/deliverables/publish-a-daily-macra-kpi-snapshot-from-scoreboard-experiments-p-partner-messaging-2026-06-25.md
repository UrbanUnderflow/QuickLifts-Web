# Publish a daily Macra KPI snapshot from Scoreboard, Experiments, purchase logs, cancel reasons, retargeting, and AppsFlyer coverage

## Core narrative

The daily Macra KPI snapshot is derived from the existing Scoreboard, Experiments, purchase logs, cancel reasons, retargeting state, and AppsFlyer coverage before any partner-facing messaging is sent. That means the operating picture is assembled from the real source artifacts first, and only then translated into any external summary, partner update, or funnel message so the story stays grounded in the current daily read rather than stale interpretation, isolated metrics, or unsupported assumptions.

## Messaging pillars

### 1. Scoreboard and Experiments set the daily operating frame

The daily snapshot starts with **Scoreboard** and **Experiments** because they define the top-line picture of what is changing right now. These two sources establish whether trial-start behavior is holding, improving, or softening before any partner-facing summary is written. If the daily read is mixed, the message should reflect that mixed state instead of forcing a cleaner story than the operating artifacts support.

### 2. Purchase logs, cancel reasons, and retargeting state explain the movement behind the numbers

**Purchase logs** show where conversion is actually happening, while **cancel reasons** and **retargeting state** explain whether the funnel is sustaining trust after the initial click or losing it later in the path. Together, those sources help the daily snapshot say more than "up" or "down." They clarify whether movement is tied to buying behavior, post-click hesitation, or the quality of follow-up after interest is created.

### 3. AppsFlyer coverage keeps acquisition context attached to the snapshot

**AppsFlyer coverage** keeps the daily KPI snapshot honest about traffic mix and source visibility. A strong day should not be framed as a broad partner signal if source coverage is partial or if quality is being carried by a narrower acquisition slice. Keeping AppsFlyer coverage in the pillar ensures the daily message stays tied to what we can actually attribute, not just what we want the numbers to mean.

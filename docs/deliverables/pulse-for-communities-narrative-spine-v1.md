# Pulse for Communities – Narrative Spine v1

## Immutable Core Story: Pulse as the Challenge Layer

Pulse is the challenge layer that keeps fitness communities showing up together. It sits on top of whatever platform you already use — Instagram, WhatsApp, a gym app, a run club newsletter — and turns your loose community into a rhythm of shared challenges that people actually finish. On Pulse, members don’t just “join a program”; they join a concrete challenge with a clear start and finish, see their friends and creators doing it with them, and feel the small social pressure that keeps them showing up on the days they’d normally fall off. When they hit the finish line, Pulse doesn’t drop them back into the void — it rolls them straight into the next block, so “I’ll try again someday” turns into “What’s our next one?” The result is simple: more people join challenges, more of them finish, and more come back for the next block — not because we nag them, but because the community itself becomes the habit.

# Refresh active variant_a experiment results before making funnel partner messaging

## Core narrative

Partner-facing funnel messaging must be updated only after the active `variant_a` experiment results are refreshed from the live operating inputs. That means we do not move forward on partner-facing funnel copy, campaign framing, or trial-start messaging from stale assumptions, prior narratives, or outdated snapshots. We refresh the active `variant_a` read against the live operating system first, then decide what the message is allowed to say.

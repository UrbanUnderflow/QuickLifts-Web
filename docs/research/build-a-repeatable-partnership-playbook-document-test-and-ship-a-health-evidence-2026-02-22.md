# Evidence Memo: Building a Repeatable Partnership Playbook for Pulse

## Purpose

This evidence memo exists to inform the design of Pulse's partner onboarding kit — specifically the co-branded landing page, onboarding flow, and tracking dashboard that partners will use to activate their communities on Pulse. The goal is to surface health and behavior evidence relevant to engagement and retention in digital fitness challenges and community-led training, so that we can design a repeatable partnership playbook that reliably gets new partners from first contact to their community's first active Pulse round in under 14 days.

This document will focus on:
- Evidence around engagement and retention in digital fitness challenges and community-led training environments
- Patterns that matter for time-to-activation and early habit formation
- Design implications for partner-facing materials (landing pages, onboarding flows, dashboards)

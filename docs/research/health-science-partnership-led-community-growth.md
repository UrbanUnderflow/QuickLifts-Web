# Partnership-Led Community Growth — Health & Behavioral Science Synthesis

This document synthesizes health and behavioral science to support Pulse's north star of **Partnership-Led Community Growth** — becoming the digital home for the most energized, community-driven fitness brands, gyms, and run clubs. It focuses on how community structures, behavior-only metrics (showing up, closing multi-day gaps, recovery/readiness rituals), and credible human guides interact to influence adherence, motivation, and long-term retention on Pulse. Subsequent sections will distill field observations, patterns, partnership recommendations, and guardrails for claims and product design.

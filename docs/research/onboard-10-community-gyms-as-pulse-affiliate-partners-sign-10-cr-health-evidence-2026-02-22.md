# Evidence Memo: Onboarding 10 Community Gyms as Pulse Affiliate Partners

## Purpose

This evidence memo exists to inform how Pulse designs the affiliate program and in-gym activation playbook for community gyms (CrossFit boxes, cycle studios, and strength-focused facilities) so that:
- We can successfully onboard at least 10 gyms as active Pulse affiliate partners, and
- Each gym can drive ≥50% of its member base to create Pulse accounts within 60 days of onboarding.

The goal is to surface evidence from gym-based and community fitness programs about what drives member engagement, sign-up behavior, and early adoption of new tools, then translate those insights into a concrete activation strategy Pulse can offer every new affiliate.

This document will focus on:
- Evidence around engagement and retention in gym-based and community fitness environments
- Patterns that influence member adoption of new digital tools introduced through their gym
- Design implications for Pulse’s affiliate onboarding, in-gym activation flows, and coach-facing scripts to reach ≥50% member activation within 60 days

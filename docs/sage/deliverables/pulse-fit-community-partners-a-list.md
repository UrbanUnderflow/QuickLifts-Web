# Pulse-fit Community Partners A-list — Brands, Gyms/Studios, Run Clubs (Step 5/5)

**Scope.** This A-list packages shortlisted, behaviorally aligned communities into three ICP-specific pages:

- **Brands** (wearables, platforms, movement/wellness brands)
- **Gyms/Studios** (global franchises + archetypal local studios)
- **Run Clubs** (brand-affiliated and independent crews)

Each entry follows a consistent template:

- Name / archetype
- Brief ecosystem description
- Key creator & challenge behaviors (currently **Unverified**; see prior docs)
- Primary community channels
- Fit tier (Flagship-ready vs Future-fit, Hypothesis)
- One-line: **Why Pulse fits this community**

> **Evidence status.** All behavioral details are currently **Unverified** and based on general ecosystem patterns (see `pulse-fit-community-partner-behavioral-signals.md`). This A-list is for **framing and narrative targeting**, not for claiming active prospects or partnerships.

---

## 1. Brands A-list (Wearables & Platforms)

### 1.1 Nike Run Club (NRC)

- **Ecosystem:** Global run community anchored in the NRC app, with city-based run clubs, Nike coaches, and sponsored athletes leading training blocks and events.
- **Creators & Challenges (Unverified):**
  - Nike coaches and sponsored athletes produce structured training content and race-prep narratives.
  - App-based training plans and seasonal challenges tied to major races and onboarding new runners.
  - Local leaders and members share run recaps, progress, and race outcomes on social.
- **Primary Channels (Unverified):**
  - NRC mobile app (training plans, tracking, and challenges).
  - Instagram (@nikerunning and city-level handles), plus local run-club pages and email lists.
  - City-level run clubs likely supported by ad-hoc WhatsApp/FB groups.
- **Fit Tier:** **Flagship-ready (Hypothesis)**
- **Why Pulse fits this community (one line):**
  - Pulse can give NRC leaders a structured way to **track adherence and completion across multi-week run cohorts**, turning NRC’s existing training plans into measurable, creator-led challenge seasons.

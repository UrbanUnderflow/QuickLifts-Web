# Pulse Partnership Community ICP Tasks

## 1. Partnership Strategy Spine

Pulse’s next growth phase is powered by partnerships that act as true extensions of the product, not ad slots: we prioritize 3–5 strategic collaborations with fitness, wellness, and wearable brands whose products, communities, and values naturally interlock with Pulse so that every partnership amplifies our creator ecosystem, drives in-app engagement through co-created experiences, and generates revenue to fund product development while clearing a high bar for health integrity and trust.

### Core ICPs and Primary Goals for Pulse

- **Brands (supplements, apparel, wellness products)**  
  **Primary goal for Pulse:** Amplify and legitimize creator-led campaigns by pairing aligned products with credible, habit-building programming inside Pulse, so campaigns feel like integrated training arcs rather than one-off sponsorship posts.

- **Gyms & Studios**  
  **Primary goal for Pulse:** Digitize and extend their in-house communities through Pulse-powered programming, challenges, and check-ins that keep members connected to coaches and each other between sessions, increasing retention and visit frequency.

- **Run Clubs & Endurance Communities**  
  **Primary goal for Pulse:** Scale their core rituals (routes, weekly sessions, race blocks, accountability threads) into a structured, trackable digital layer that deepens community identity, improves training consistency, and opens up co-created events and challenges with Pulse creators and brand partners.

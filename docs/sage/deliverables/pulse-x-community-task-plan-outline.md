# Pulse x Community — Artifact Outline (Step 1/5)

This document outlines the required artifacts for the Pulse x Community strategy. Each artifact description includes its purpose and primary usage contexts (decks, outreach, one-pagers).

---

## 1. Pulse x Community Core Narrative Spine

**Purpose:** Define the shared story that explains how Pulse turns existing communities (brands, gyms, run clubs) into living, trackable training ecosystems — in language that feels like a real coach/creator, not a brand deck.

**Usage:** Anchor narrative for partnership decks, the "why Pulse" section in outreach emails, and the opening paragraph of any one-pager or landing section about community partnerships.

---

## 2. Pulse x Community KPI Specification

**Purpose:** Specify the handful of core metrics that prove Pulse is working for a community (e.g., challenge join/finish/return rates, creator activation, streak weeks), with clear definitions and example baselines.

**Usage:** Performance and ROI slides in partner decks, internal measurement docs, and any partner one-pagers or follow-up emails that need to translate "vibes" into numbers.

---

## 3. Partner-Fit Rubric (Pulse-fit Community Partners)

**Purpose:** Create a concise rubric for evaluating whether a potential partner (brand, gym/studio, run club) is truly Pulse-fit, based on observable behaviors and community patterns rather than vibes or follower counts.

**Usage:** Internal screening tool for prospect lists, a one-slide summary in decks to show selectivity, and a reference in outreach to signal that Pulse cares about community quality, not just reach.

---

## 4. ICP-Specific Document — Brands

**Purpose:** Translate the core narrative and KPIs into a brands-specific story that shows how Pulse powers creator-led drops, challenges, and UGC that make the brand's community feel alive (not just sponsored posts).

**Usage:** Brand partnership decks (brands track), brand-specific one-pagers, and outbound/inbound outreach templates to supplement the core narrative with brand-relevant examples and metrics.

---

## 5. ICP-Specific Document — Gyms & Studios

**Purpose:** Translate the core narrative and KPIs into a gyms/studios story that focuses on coach-led blocks, check-ins, and member retention — how Pulse turns programming and accountability into a tangible community heartbeat.

**Usage:** Gym/studio partnership decks, gym-focused one-pagers, and outreach to owners or head coaches who care about filled blocks, return rates, and keeping their best people engaged.

---

## 6. ICP-Specific Document — Run Clubs

**Purpose:** Translate the core narrative and KPIs into a run clubs story that centers on seasons, routes, races, and streak weeks — how Pulse captures the rhythm of a club and keeps runners coming back.

**Usage:** Run club partnership decks, route/season-focused one-pagers, and outreach to club leaders who want more consistent participation and a cleaner way to see who shows up, trains, and returns.

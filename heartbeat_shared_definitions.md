# Heartbeat Shared Definitions and Preflight Checklist

## 1. Heartbeat Colors
- **Color Name**: Description of usage and meaning.
  - Example: **Red**: Indicates high urgency and immediate action required.
  
## 2. Narrative Lenses
- **Lens Name**: Brief description of how this lens shapes narratives.
  - Example: **Health Lens**: Focuses on metrics related to physical activity and overall health.

## 3. Beat Types
- **Beat Type**: Definition and purpose of the beat type.
  - Example: **Check-in Beat**: A regular update on team progress and individual tasks.
